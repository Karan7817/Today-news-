How It Works

Our background remover tool uses advanced AI algorithms to automatically detect and remove backgrounds from your images. Just follow these simple steps:

  1. Upload your image by dragging and dropping or clicking to browse
  2. Select your preferred compression level and output format
  3. Click "Remove Background" and wait a few seconds
  4. Download your image with transparent background

The entire process happens in your browser for maximum privacy and speed. We don't store your images on our servers.
